# Color

## Contrast theory

- high contrast creates emphasis and urgency
- moderate contrast balances readability and comfort
- low contrast should be used carefully where softness matters more than emphasis

## Palette systems

- define neutrals, primary accents, and semantic colors
- use color for meaning, grouping, and attention guidance
- avoid decorative color changes that do not support hierarchy

## Medium awareness

- RGB is screen-first
- CMYK is print-first
- print output may shift from digital previews, so proof when it matters

## Accessibility

- ensure text contrast remains readable
- do not rely on color alone to communicate state or meaning
