# Product Thinking Rule

## Always Ask

- What problem is being solved?
- Is there a simpler solution?
- What will confuse the user?
- What is the fastest path to value?

## Priorities

1. clarity
2. usability
3. speed
4. scalability
5. polish

## Avoid

- overengineering
- unnecessary features
- complex flows without reason

## For dashboards

- optimize for scanning
- reduce cognitive load
- highlight key actions

## For workflows

- must be understandable visually
- logic must be predictable
